The Mission

Play is medicine for the soul.

We believe play is not a break from real life — it's one of the only things that reaches every single person the same way, regardless of what their body or their bank account can do. Our mission is simple: give every kid and adult, of every ability, at least one day where they play like a champion, because someone built the day to make sure they could.

The Problem

Sports have a sideline. We don't think play should.

Traditional sports were built around who's fastest, strongest, or already able. That works for some. It leaves out a lot of people: kids with disabilities, adaptive athletes, military families rebuilding a sense of normal, kids who just don't fit the mold — everyone the scoreboard was never designed for. They don't need a modified version of somebody else's game. They need a game built for them from the first draw of the court.

The Vision

A day nobody forgets, in every city that needs one.

We started in Colorado Springs — Olympic City USA. We're building toward Play It Forward Day running in schools, military bases, and communities nationwide, funded by a growing community of Playmakers, so that "anytime, anywhere, with anyone" isn't a slogan. It's a schedule.
