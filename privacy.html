Privacy Policy

Effective date: to be set at launch.

What we collect

When you donate, become a Playmaker, fill out a form, or contact us, we collect the information you give us: name, email, and — for donations — the billing details our payment processor (Zeffy) needs to complete your gift. We do not store your card details on our servers.

How we use it

We use your information to process your gift, send your tax receipt, share the updates you asked for, and respond when you reach out. You can change what you hear about — or stop hearing from us — with one click in any email.

What we never do

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Donor information is shared only with the service providers who make our work possible (payment processing, email delivery), and only to the extent needed to do that work.

Children

Our site is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect their personal information. Stories and photos of program participants appear only with a parent or guardian's written consent.
