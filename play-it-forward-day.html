Signature Program

The day every underdog gets a red carpet, a game-winning shot, and a jersey with their name on it.

Play It Forward Day is our signature all-abilities experience for families with special needs, military families, and underserved schools — built around one idea: everyone in this family is about to feel like a champion, together.

Bring it to your community Fund a day

We didn't build a modified version of somebody else's event. We built one from scratch, around a single question: what would it feel like to be picked first, for once? Here's what that day looks like.

1. Play As a Team

Families — or randomly paired teammates — take on a red-carpet challenge course together, coached by our Play Architects. Before anyone takes their shot, everyone plays as one squad.

2. The Championship Moment

Each underdog gets a one-on-one coaching session to unlock their game-winning shot. When it lands, the crowd loses it — carried on shoulders, mobbed at center court, whatever the moment calls for. Then comes the jersey: a custom, screen-printed shirt with their name on it, signed on the spot in front of everyone — the same way a recruit signs on to play for a college team. Except this time, they're signing in as an Air O Sport Champion.

3. Eat Like a Champion

The whole family sits down together for a feast afterward. Before they leave, we send them home with weekly and monthly play-powered challenges, so the momentum doesn't stop when the day does — and every champion becomes a standing member of the Air O Sport Championship Squad.
