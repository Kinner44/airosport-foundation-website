Meet the Team

The people who make sure we build this right.

A founder can invent a sport. It takes a board like this to build a movement.

PHOTO: Mark Lucas headshot

Mark Lucas — Board Chair

Champion for Adaptive Athletes

Mark spent his career as Executive Director of the US Association of Blind Athletes and helped pioneer Goalball in America — the sport, and the belief behind it. He carried the Olympic Torch at the 1996 Atlanta Games. He's forgotten more about adaptive sport than most people ever learn, and he keeps us honest about building it right.

PHOTO: Nichole Rotte headshot

Nichole Rotte

Champion for Military Families & Wounded Warriors

Nichole served 12 years as a U.S. Army Engineer Officer and led leadership development at West Point, training future officers. She built her unit's Family Readiness Group from scratch to help military families navigate deployment. She knows exactly what a military family needs on the hardest days — and how to build them a great one.

PHOTO: Matt Sprang headshot

Matt Sprang

Champion for All Sports & Leadership Growth

Matt spent 20 years as CEO and head coach of a swim club and swim school before becoming a business coach and consultant who now advises CEOs and leads facilitation for Front Row Dads, a national community for engaged fathers. He's coached everyone from age-group swimmers to company presidents on the same idea: growth needs a coach in your corner.

PHOTO: Aaron Groziak headshot

Aaron Groziak

Champion for Veterans

Aaron runs business operations for the Colorado Veterans Business Outreach Center, helping veterans build real, sustainable businesses across the state. He makes sure every veteran and military family we serve gets treated like the asset they are — not a case file.
