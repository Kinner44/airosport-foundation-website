Accessibility

Everyone plays. That includes this website.

A foundation built on all-abilities play cannot ship a site that leaves anyone out. Accessibility is not a checkbox for us — it's the mission, applied to pixels.

Our commitment

We build to WCAG 2.1 AA: semantic structure for screen readers, full keyboard navigation with visible focus states, color contrast that passes AA, alt text on every meaningful image, and captions on every video we publish.

Where we are honest about the work

We're a young foundation and this site will keep evolving. If anything here doesn't work with your screen reader, keyboard, switch device, or anything else you use — that's a bug in our mission, not just our website.

Tell us and we'll fix it

Email mike@playairosport.com with what happened and what you were using. A human reads these, and fixes are prioritized ahead of new features.
